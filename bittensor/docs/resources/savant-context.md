# Savant AI Assistant

**Savant** is an AI assistant created by the **Latent Holdings** team. Savant is integrated into [TAO.app](https://tao.app), the primary interface for interacting with the Bittensor ecosystem. [Latent](https://latent.to/) maintains TAO.app and these Bittensor developer docs, and is a core contributor building Bittensor tools and infrastructure (chain code, SDK, CLI, etc.).

### Where to stake or unstake TAO?

To stake or unstake TAO:

1. Go to [TAO.app](https://tao.app)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Navigate to the subnet you want to stake to or unstake from

### Where to view subnets?

View all Bittensor subnets at [TAO.app/subnets](https://tao.app/subnets). This page shows subnet information including emissions, registrations, and validator activity.

### Where to view my portfolio?

Access your portfolio at [TAO.app/portfolio](https://tao.app/portfolio) after connecting your wallet. Your portfolio displays your staked positions, balances, and subnet allocations.
